Service

Paris

Ascend the iconic Eiffel Tower for stunning city views or dine at its acclaimed restaurants. Explore world-class art and history at the Louvre, home to the Mona Lisa and countless treasures. Enjoy a romantic Seine cruise, passing Notre-Dame and other landmarks. Discover artistic Montmartre's charm, with Sacré-Cœur and cobblestone streets.


Switzerland

Experience Switzerland's majestic Alps, ski pristine slopes, and enjoy traditional mountain villages on our thrilling Alpine Adventure. Dive into Swiss history and culture in Zurich, Basel, and Geneva with our Historic Cities tour. Satisfy your taste buds with Swiss chocolates, cheese fondue, and fine wines on our delectable Culinary Delights journey. Traverse Switzerland's beauty via iconic trains, witnessing lakes, mountains, and viaducts on our Scenic Rail Tour.


Dubai

Embark on a thrilling adventure in the Arabian desert with our Dubai Desert Safari. Enjoy heart-pounding dune bashing, camel rides, and traditional entertainment against the backdrop of stunning sunsets. Elevate your Dubai experience by visiting the Burj Khalifa's At the Top observation deck, offering unparalleled views of the city's skyline from the world's tallest building. Cruise the dazzling waters of Dubai Marina, surrounded by glittering skyscrapers, and relish a delectable dinner while taking in the breathtaking views of the illuminated cityscape. Explore The Dubai Mall, a shopper's paradise offering luxury brands, unique souvenirs, and an opportunity to visit the Dubai Aquarium, all while enjoying international cuisine in this colossal retail haven.


Nepal

Explore Nepal's stunning landscapes, from Himalayan peaks to lush jungles. Trek iconic trails like Annapurna or immerse in Pokhara's tranquility while embracing Nepali culture. Dive into Kathmandu's vibrant culture with ancient temples, bustling markets, and sacred sites like Swayambhunath and Boudhanath Stupa. Nepal beckons adventurers with activities like white-water rafting, paragliding, and wildlife safaris in Chitwan National Park. Whatever your thrill, Nepal has it all.
